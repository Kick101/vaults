### Chat GPT Suggestions
#### 🧘‍♀️ **Posture Check-In**

- Subtle reminders to sit upright, unclench jaw, relax shoulders.
- Could use phone sensors (or webcam prompt) to encourage awareness.

#### 🧠 Mental & Focus Hacks
- **Pomodoro Technique**: Work for 25 minutes, take a 5-minute break. After 4 sessions, take a 15–30 min break. Apps like **Focus To-Do** or **Forest** help with this.
- **Mindful Breathing Breaks**: Use apps like **Breethe**, **Headspace**, or **Oak** for short 1–3 minute breathing exercises between tasks.
- **"Two-Minute Rule"**: If something takes less than 2 minutes, do it immediately (keeps your mind clear).

#### 🌿  Ambient Mood Checks
- Quick "mood polls" or "how’s your energy?" with emoji scale.
- Over time, the app can show trends or correlations (e.g. hydration + energy).

#### 🌞 Natural Light Reminder
- Prompt to step outside or look out a window for natural light exposure (helps circadian rhythm).

#### 🧩 Customization Layers
- Let users choose goal focus: _"Vision care," "Hydration boost," "Mindful productivity."_
- Personalize reminder frequency and tone (so it feels supportive, not annoying).

#### 🛠️ Automation Hacks

- **Smart Reminders**: Use tools like **IFTTT** or **Shortcuts (iOS)** to automate hydration + break + eye care reminders.
- **Slack/Teams Bots**: Some bots can remind you to stretch or hydrate directly within work apps.
