# 🧭 Threat Explorer

![[Pasted image 20250703125221.png]]

**Real-time report** that allows you to identify and analyze recent threats. You can configure Explorer to show data for custom periods.

- Inside Threat Explorer, you're first shown the variety of threat families impacting our organization over time. Additionally, you're shown the top threats and top targeted users inside the organization.
- Central dashboard for **real-time analysis of email threats**
    
**Filter the Threat Explorer graph by:**
- Threat family
- Detection technology (e.g., sandbox, Exchange Online Protection (EOP))
- Sender/recipient

**It shows:**
- Malware behaviors
- Delivery actions (blocked/quarantined/delivered)
- Targeted users
- Message traces, sender IPs, and subject lines
