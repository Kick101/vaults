Microsoft Defender XDR introduces a **Unified RBAC model** that consolidates role/permission management across:

- Defender for Endpoint (MDE)
- Defender Vulnerability Management (MDVM)
- Defender for Office 365 (MDO)
- Defender for Identity (MDI)
- Microsoft Entra (formerly Azure AD)
