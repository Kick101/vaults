### 🧠 How it Works:

- **Next-Gen Protection**:  
    Detects and halts threats _based on behavior_ (even if the file looks clean).
    
- **EDR**:  
    *Collects signals from:*
    - OS kernel
    - Devices
    - Network
    - Identities & apps  
        Groups related alerts into **incidents** for easier investigation.
        
- **Microsoft Defender XDR Integration**:  
    Correlates data across Defender solutions to provide complete attack visibility and detection.

## Feedback-loop blocking works

Once malware is confirmed:

- It is **instantly blocked**:
    - On the original device
    - On other **devices in the org**
    - On devices in **other orgs** via cloud intelligence

### EDR: Block Mode

Defender for Endpoint blocks malicious artifacts or behaviors that are observed through post-breach protection. EDR in block mode works behind the scenes to remediate malicious artifacts that are detected post-breach.
