| Module           | Focus                                | Goal                                 |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------ |
| **DLP Alerts**   | Policy violations (e.g., data leaks) | Prevent data exfiltration            |
| **Insider Risk** | Risky user behavior                  | Detect & mitigate insider threats    |
| **Audit**        | Log of all actions                   | Investigate activity post-incident   |
| **eDiscovery**   | Legal data collection                | Respond to legal/compliance requests |

>To focus only on DLP-related alerts, filter by **Service source: Data Loss Prevention**.

>If your organization shares Insider Risk Management signals with Defender, you can also see the user's insider risk severity level next to their DLP alerts. This helps prioritize which alerts might require immediate action.
