## 📬  Email Notification Types

|**Notification Type**|**Purpose**|**Triggers When**|
|---|---|---|
|**Incidents**|Notifies about incident creation|New **Incidents** are created|
|**Threat Analytics**|Notifies about new threat intelligence|New **Threat Analytics reports** are published|

## ⚙️ Configure Incident Email Notifications

1. **Go to:** `Settings > Microsoft Defender XDR > Email notifications`
2. Select **Incidents**
3. Click **Add incident email notification**
4. Provide:
    - **Name & description**
    - **Device/alert criteria**
    - **Recipient email(s)**
5. Click **Create rule**

## 🛡 Configure Threat Analytics Email Notifications

1. **Go to:** `Settings > Microsoft Defender XDR > Email notifications`
2. Select **Threat Analytics**
3. Click **Create a notification rule**
4. Provide:
    - **Name & description**
    - **Threat analytics criteria (tags/types)**
    - **Recipient email(s)**
5. Click **Create rule**
