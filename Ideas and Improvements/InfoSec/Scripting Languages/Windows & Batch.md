###  Batch Scripting

|command|Description|
|--|--|
| `@`|silent prompt for a command|
|`@echo off`|Turn Off echo|
|`echo on`|Turn On echo|
|`set name=kickass`|declare variables|
|`set`|display environment variables|
|`echo %name%`|display variable values|
|`echo ^<html^>`|escape character|
|`set /a sum=1+1`|`/a` for mathematical interpretation|
