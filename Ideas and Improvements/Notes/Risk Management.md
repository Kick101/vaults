__Risk Management:__
Risk management is a risk assessment method that analyzes and eliminates risks to mitigate threats and optimize an investment’s profits. Risk management includes the detection, review, and reaction to risk factors that are part of a company’s existence.
https://www.eccouncil.org/risk-management/

__What is a risk?__
The `chance` that an outcome or investment's actual gains will differ from an expected outcome or return

__Cyber Security Risk Management Process:__
- Identify
- Analyze
- Evaluate
- Prioritize
- Treat
- Monitor
