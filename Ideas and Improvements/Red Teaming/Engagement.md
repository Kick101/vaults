__Engagement phases or Attack Kill Chain__
1. Planning  
2. Doing  
3. Reporting

The engagement begins by performing __external reconnaissance__
- public-facing applications, 
- IP ranges, 
- domain names, 
- technologies and products used, 
- employees, 
- organisational structure, 
- service providers, 
- suppliers and more. 

This information is then _used to plan an attack_ on the perimeter.
Once a foothold has been obtained, the team will perform __internal reconnaissance__
- network topology,
- internal systems
- processes and defensive products & capabilities. 

They may also install backdoors on the foothold(s) to ensure they can maintain persistent access to the environment.
