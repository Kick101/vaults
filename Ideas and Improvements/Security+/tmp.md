__Basic Ques__
1. When was the last time you used your computer and why?
2. What interests you in the field of cybersecurity?
3. Have tried anything related to hacking if so, what tools did you use? Or have you heard about any hacking tools/techniques?
4. Have you participated in any computer related competitions? If so, What was your experience?
5. Do you think cybersecurity is important? Explain.
6. What is your main motive to join the club?
7. How do you think the club can help raise awareness about cybersecurity in the community?
8.  Have you or anyone that you know been a victim of a cybercrime? If so, tell me about it. And, how could you have avoided it?
9. If you have interest in the cybersecurity, what stopped you from learning it?
10. What do you want to do if you become a hacker?

__Basic Computer & Network Ques__
1. Name few computer hardware devices like input/output devices and network devices?
2. What is the difference between hardware and software?
3. Define network. What are the types of network?
4. Can you explain the difference between RAM and ROM?
5. Can you explain the difference between a CPU and a GPU?
6. What are some common file extensions and what do they indicate about the file type?
7. Have you heard about OSI or TCP/IP model? If so, explain briefly about them.
8. What is the need of a VPN?
9. Can you explain the difference between private and public IP?
10. Have you heard about Dark/Deep Web? Explain.
11. Have you heard the term subnet? Explain.

3. How do you protect yourself from phishing scams?
4. Name few types of cyber attacks.

6. How can you tell if a website is legitimate or a scam?
7. What is a virus?
