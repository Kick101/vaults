### Morning Routine
- Fill your mouth with water; expands eye muscles
- Splash cold water on eyes
- Fill eye wash cup with 15 drops of pure rose water and fill rest with cold water
- Put eye in eye wash cup for 1 min; repeat with another eye

### Eye Tonic
- 7 soaked and peeled Gurbandi almonds
- 4 seeds of black pepper 
- Smash them to smooth paste
- Boil 250ml cow milk for 10min on low flame
- Do NOT eat for next 1hr
